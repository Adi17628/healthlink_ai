**HealthLink AI**

## 1. Overview  

HealthLink AI is a user-friendly AI-powered health assistant that allows users to query **symptoms** and **medications** through text or image-based inputs.  
It leverages **Google's Gemini API** to offer general first aid guidance, self-care tips, and medication insights, making healthcare information accessible anytime, anywhere.  

---

## 2. Target Users  

- Students and Working Professionals seeking quick medical insights  
- Rural and Semi-urban Populations with limited access to doctors  
- Parents and Caregivers managing children or elderly health  
- Fitness and Wellness Enthusiasts looking for minor condition help  

---

## 3. Value Proposition  

- Accessible anytime health support  
- No prior medical knowledge required  
- Simple interface with text/image input options  
- Fast, AI-generated responses  
- Secure and private: No health data stored permanently  

---

## 4. User Acquisition Channels  

### A. Direct Channels  
- College Events: Present our team app at fests  

### B. Digital Channels  
- Influencer Collaborations: Partner with fitness/health micro-influencers  
- App Showcase on GitHub/Reddit: Share in relevant developer and health-tech communities  
- SEO-Optimized Blog: Educational content around common illnesses and medications  

### C. Community Outreach  
- Workshops in Schools/Colleges: Explain safe use of AI in health  

---

## 5. Onboarding Strategy  

- First-time Tutorial: Walkthrough on how to ask queries or upload images  
- Sample Queries: Built-in examples for symptom and medicine search  
- API Key Guidance: Step-by-step to get and use Gemini API Key
